About Us:

Isshin Benkyo was established in June 2022 after the owner decided to realize her dreams of opening a stationery shop. Asian stationery has always been a passion of hers since she first discovered the field as a 7th grader. The store strives to provide the best quality stationery to allow clients to reach their full potential, hence the company name which can be translated to mean 'put one's whole heart into studying'.
